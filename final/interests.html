music and hobbies are the threads that weave together my life, forming a tapestry of art, academics, and purpose. tchaikovsky’s symphonies relate to me not just in music but in ballet, while joe hisaishi’s scores for studio ghibli evoke the magic and wonder of nature and simplicity. chloe chua and yo-yo ma inspire me to pursue excellence in my own music, finding harmony between techinque and artistry.

as for my hobbies, ballet and cello introduce me to countless communities and hold the keys to so many opportunities . math challenges me to think beyond the obvious, pushing me to explore new possibilities, while my environmental advocacy work for the southern resident orcas drives me to protect the earth that is our world. each of these interests shapes me, helping me grow to become who i someday strive to be.
